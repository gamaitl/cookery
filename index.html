What is Mayca?

Mayca is a smashing sweet spread made out of goats milk, Papantla vanilla and other Mexican spices.

  • It's gluten free.
  • Its palm oil free.
  • Its cow milk free.
  • Its vegetarian.
  • Its chocolate free.
Hence having half the calorie intake of most jams, marmalades and chocolate spreads. You get the benefits from the protein present in goats. (Click here to find out what they are).

  • Made from 100% Pasteurized Goats Milk, raised in pastures in the Laguna Valley, in Coahuila, México.
  • Healthy Pure Wholesome Energy to get you started the right way.
  • 100% Pure Cane Sugar.
